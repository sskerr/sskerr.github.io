Sarah Stein Kerr

Multimedia Journalist


About


Sarah Stein Kerr is a multimedia journalist based in New York City specializing in video and interactive journalism.

Sarah previously worked at WITNESS, an international human rights organization focused on video as a tool for human rights documentation and storytelling. Sarah is currently a student at the CUNY Graduate School of Journalism.

CV

Education

CUNY Graduate School of Journalism

Masters of Arts, expected graduation: 12/2017 Concentrations: social video, video production, urban reporting.

McGill University, Montréal, Québec, Canada

Bachelor of Arts, Joint Honors International Development & Political Science. 2007-2011. Studied in Tunisia (Fall 2011).

Journalism and Media Experience

Freelance Journalist, September 2016 – Present

Print and multimedia reporter focused on the northwest Bronx.

Freelance Video and Film Producer, February 2014 - Present

  • Produce, shoot, and edit original video content for the web. Clients include The 2016 Democratic National Convention, If Not Now (Jewish youth movement working to end the occupation in the West Bank), UnHaul (web doc series on sustainable fashion).

  • Producer, INAATE/SE/, feature length documentary on contemporary indigenous identity in North America. Closing night film at the Museum of Modern Art’s Documentary Fortnight Festival, 2016.

Program and Communications Coordinator, WITNESS, Brooklyn, NY, May 2013 – October 2016

WITNESS is a human rights organization focused on video as a tool for citizen journalism, documentation, and advocacy.

  • Editor and writer for WITNESS blog focused on video, technology, and activism (avg. 10,000 unique readers per month). Launched organization’s publication on Medium.

  • Content creator and engagement specialist for the WITNESS Media Lab’s social video projects on Western Sahara, police violence in the US and ethical guidelines for user generated content.

  • Digital engagement lead for WITNESS’ work on tech policy, video as evidence, and regional work in Africa, Asia, and the Middle East and North Africa.

  • Trained international groups and staff on video production, social video verification, and digital security.

Popular
