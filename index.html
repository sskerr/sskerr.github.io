About


Sarah Stein Kerr is a multimedia journalist specializing in video and interactive journalism.

Before transitioning to journalism, Sarah worked at WITNESS, an international human rights organization focused on video as a tool for human rights documentation and storytelling. Sarah worked on projects on user-generated video verification and curation, police violence, evidentiary video and digital security.

Sarah was a 2014-2015 collaborative fellow at UnionDocs , a documentary arts center in Brooklyn, N.Y. where she produced short experimental documentaries.

Originally from Saint Paul, Minnesota, Sarah holds a joint honors degree in political science and international development studies from McGill University in Montreal, QC.

Sarah is currently a student at the CUNY Graduate School of Journalism and is based in New York City. Sarah's full CV is available here.
